Step outside your shoes
We Humans.
We are
privilege
seeking
creatures.
We
are
engulfed in
a
bubble that
we've
created for
ourselves,
where,
we
compare
each
other,
based on,
not
what we
are or
do,
but what we
possess and
how
we are
perceived.
We, humans,
are
stupid.
Step outside
your
shoes.
Go places.
Meet
people.
Drop
your
material
attachments.
Garner
experiences
that make
you
richer.
Collect
smiles
from
people
you
help. Talk
to
people. Talk
to the
earth.
Don't do it
for
an
instagram
moment, or a
facebook
like.
Just
do
it – for it.
Become
better.
Stop
complaining.
Invent.
Reinvent.
Live and
Love.
